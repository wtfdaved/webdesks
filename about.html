WebDesks is a suite of 18 browser-based productivity tools built for people who want to focus, create, and get things done — without subscriptions, accounts, or software to install.

Our Mission

Productivity software has a price problem. The most useful tools — task managers, timers, PDF editors, screen recorders, noise generators — are scattered across a dozen different apps, each charging a monthly subscription. A knowledge worker who wants access to all of them could easily spend $100+ per month just to have the basics covered.

We built WebDesks on a different belief: the tools you need to do your best work should be freely available to everyone, not just those who can afford to pay for them. Students studying for exams, freelancers building their businesses, remote workers collaborating across time zones, and office professionals looking to regain their focus — they all deserve the same quality of tools.

WebDesks is supported by advertising (Google AdSense), not subscriptions. The ads are how we keep the lights on. The tools are, and always will be, completely free.

Built with Privacy First

We made a deliberate architectural choice when building WebDesks: every tool that processes your personal files or data does so entirely within your browser. The PDF Merger uses the open-source pdf-lib library — your documents never touch our servers. The Screen Recorder uses your browser's native MediaRecorder API — video goes directly to your device.

Your notes, tasks, timer settings, and whiteboard drawings are stored in your browser's localStorage — on your device, under your control. We have no access to this data. If you clear your browser storage, it's gone — because it was always yours.

The only data that flows through external services is what you'd expect from any website: server access logs, anonymized analytics, and the advertising pixels required by Google AdSense (which funds the free service). These are covered in detail in our Privacy Policy.
