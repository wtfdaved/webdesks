Focus Town: Earn Buildings by Staying Focused — Gamified Productivity in Your Browser

By WebDesks Team · 8 min read · Productivity

Staying focused is hard. Staying focused because you want to is even harder. What if each focus session made something tangible grow — a building, a town, a city? That's the idea behind Focus Town: gamified productivity that turns your work time into a growing virtual world.

Focus Town is built directly into WebDesks. Complete focus sessions using the Pomodoro Timer and watch your town evolve from a humble campsite all the way to a thriving metropolis. No coins to buy, no app to install — just work, and your town grows.

Start building: Open Focus Town — your campsite is waiting. Complete your first focus session to unlock the Hamlet stage.

What Is Focus Town?

Focus Town is a pixel-art animated scene that evolves based on how many minutes of focused work you accumulate. Each completed focus session adds minutes to your town's total, unlocking new buildings and progressing through stages of urban development.

The town has its own name (which you can customise), a day/night cycle, animated weather you can change (☀️ clear, 🌧️ rain, ❄️ snow, 🌫️ fog), and a progress bar showing how close you are to the next stage. A daily streak counter tracks consecutive days of focusing — a powerful habit-building metric.

The Focus Town Milestones

Your town grows through a series of stages, each unlocked by accumulating focus minutes:

⛺
Campsite — Starting stage. A tent in an empty field. The beginning of something great.
🌾
Hamlet — Unlocked at 10 minutes. A few humble buildings appear. The foundation of a community.
🏡
Village — Unlocked at 20 minutes. Houses, paths, and more activity. The town is taking shape.
🏘️
Town — A proper settlement with multiple buildings and infrastructure. Feeling productive yet?
🏙️
City & Beyond — Keep focusing to unlock larger urban stages. The longer you work, the more your city flourishes.

Each stage is a visual reward for real work done. The progress bar beneath the scene shows exactly how many minutes remain to the next unlock — creating the kind of "one more session" motivation that keeps you going.

The Science Behind Gamified Productivity

Extrinsic rewards boost intrinsic motivation (when done right)

Research in behavioral psychology shows that well-designed extrinsic rewards — particularly progress-based ones — enhance rather than undermine intrinsic motivation. The key is that rewards must be contingent on effort, not outcome. Focus Town nails this: the reward (building growth) is directly proportional to the work you do, not dependent on external validation.

The Zeigarnik effect and progress bars

The Zeigarnik effect states that people remember incomplete tasks better than completed ones. A progress bar showing "8 minutes to next stage" creates an open loop in your brain — a mild psychological tension that makes you want to close it. This is why seeing "90% to Hamlet" makes it surprisingly hard to close the tab.

Streaks as a habit formation tool

Daily streak counters are one of the most effective habit-formation mechanisms in app design (see: Duolingo, Snapchat). The fear of breaking a streak — loss aversion — is a powerful motivator. Focus Town's streak counter creates a daily commitment that compound over weeks into a genuine productivity habit.

How to Use Focus Town Effectively

  1. Name your town — click the town name and personalise it. "Sarah's Town" or "Deep Work HQ" — whatever creates a sense of ownership.
  2. Choose your weather — set the weather to match your mood or environment. Rain for cozy focus sessions, snow for a winter study aesthetic.
  3. Start a Pomodoro session — switch to the Focus Timer and begin a 25-minute session. When it completes, your focus minutes accumulate automatically.
  4. Watch your town grow — check back on Focus Town between sessions to see new buildings appear. The visual change is the reward.
  5. Protect your streak — complete at least one focus session every day to maintain your streak counter. Even one short session keeps the chain alive.
  6. Set a milestone goal — before your work session, decide which milestone you're aiming for today. Seeing the milestone label unlocked at the end of the day is satisfying in a way that a checkbox can't match.

Focus Town vs. Forest App and Other Gamified Focus Tools

Forest is the most well-known gamified focus app — you plant a tree that grows while you focus and dies if you use your phone. Focus Town takes a similar concept and expands it: instead of a single tree, you're building an entire town. And unlike Forest, Focus Town is:

  • Completely free — no premium tier, no in-app purchases
  • Browser-based — no app install, works on any device with a browser
  • Integrated — lives alongside all your other productivity tools in WebDesks
  • No account required — your town data saves locally in your browser

Pairing Focus Town with the Rest of WebDesks

Focus Town becomes most powerful as part of a complete productivity workflow:

  • Task Manager — start each work session by picking a task. Focus Town grows while you work; the Task Manager shows what you accomplished.
  • Pomodoro Timer — Focus Town tracks minutes from your Pomodoro sessions. Every completed 25-minute block contributes to town growth.
  • Breathing Guide — use a 2-minute breathing exercise between Pomodoro sessions instead of browsing social media. Return to Focus Town refreshed for the next block.
  • Audio Mixer — layer ambient sounds (rain, coffee shop, brown noise) during sessions for an immersive focus environment that complements Focus Town's weather atmosphere.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Focus Town free?

Yes, completely free. No account, no subscription, no in-app purchases — just open your browser and start building.

Is my town data saved?

Yes. Your focus minutes, streak, and town stage are all saved to your browser's localStorage. They persist between sessions as long as you don't clear browser data. The data is local to your device — it doesn't sync across browsers or devices.

What happens if I reset my progress?

The Reset button in Focus Town clears all your accumulated focus minutes, returning your town to the Campsite stage. This also resets your streak. Use it only if you want a fresh start — it's irreversible.

Does my town grow automatically while the timer runs?

Focus minutes accumulate when you complete Pomodoro sessions in the Focus Timer. The growth is attributed after each completed session — giving you a visible reward for finishing the full block.

Can I use Focus Town on mobile?

Yes. WebDesks is a responsive PWA that works on phones and tablets. Install it as a PWA from your mobile browser for an app-like experience with the full Focus Town animation.
