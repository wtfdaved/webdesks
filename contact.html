Common Questions

Are my files safe?

Yes — all file processing (PDF merge, media conversion, screen recording) happens entirely in your browser. Files never touch our servers.

Is WebDesks free forever?

Yes. WebDesks is supported by non-intrusive advertising and will remain free with no paywalls or subscriptions.

Can I use WebDesks offline?

After the first visit, most tools work offline thanks to the Service Worker cache. Internet is needed to load CDN-hosted libraries on first visit.
