Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Privacy-first by design. WebDesks tools (PDF Merger, Screen Recorder, Media Converter, etc.) process all files and data entirely within your browser. Nothing you work on is ever uploaded to our servers. This privacy policy primarily covers website analytics, cookies, and our use of Google AdSense advertising.

1. Who We Are

WebDesks (webdesks.app) is a free, browser-based productivity suite offering over 20 tools including a Pomodoro timer, PDF merger, screen recorder, audio mixer, and more. WebDesks is operated as an independent project. For questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@webdesks.app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We do not require account registration and we do not collect personal information (name, email, etc.) simply to use WebDesks tools. The Contact Us form collects your name, email address, and message solely to respond to your inquiry.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit WebDesks, certain information is collected automatically by our hosting provider and third-party services:

  • IP address — collected by our hosting infrastructure for security and abuse prevention.
  • Browser type and version — used to identify compatibility issues.
  • Referring URL — the page you visited before arriving at WebDesks.
  • Pages visited and time spent — used to understand which tools are most useful.
  • Device type — desktop, tablet, or mobile, for responsive design improvements.

2.3 LocalStorage Data

WebDesks stores your preferences and tool data (tasks, notes, timer settings, themes) in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device. It is not accessible to us and is deleted when you clear your browser's site data.

3. Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. We use cookies in the following ways:

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are set in response to your actions such as setting your privacy preferences or navigating between tools.

3.2 Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics services to understand how visitors interact with WebDesks. These cookies collect aggregate, anonymised information (e.g., page views, popular tools) and do not identify you personally.

3.3 Advertising Cookies — Google AdSense and DoubleClick

WebDesks uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses the DoubleClick cookie to serve ads based on your prior visits to WebDesks and other websites across the internet.

The DoubleClick cookie enables Google and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to WebDesks and/or other sites on the internet. Google uses this cookie to improve ad relevance and measure ad performance.

Specifically, Google AdSense may:

  • Use the DoubleClick cookie to track ad impressions and clicks on WebDesks.
  • Combine data from your visit with data from other websites you have visited to show interest-based advertising.
  • Share aggregated, non-personal statistics about ad performance with advertisers.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information about how Google uses data when you visit sites that use Google services, see Google's Privacy & Terms.

4. Third-Party Services

WebDesks integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdSense — advertising platform. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — font delivery. Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN; Google may log request metadata.
  • open-meteo.com — weather data for the Dashboard widget. No personal data is shared.
  • Tailwind CSS CDN / unpkg.com — CSS and JavaScript library delivery. These CDNs may log IP addresses as part of standard request logging.
  • pdf-lib (via CDN) — PDF processing library that runs entirely in your browser. No data is transmitted.
  • FFmpeg.wasm — media conversion library that runs entirely in your browser. No data is transmitted.

5. How We Use Your Information

The limited information collected is used to:

  • Operate and maintain the WebDesks website.
  • Understand usage patterns to improve our tools.
  • Serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense to fund free access to WebDesks.
  • Respond to your support requests or contact form submissions.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With advertising partners — Google AdSense receives cookie and usage data as described in Section 3.3.
  • For legal compliance — if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • To protect rights — if necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of WebDesks or its users.

7. Data Retention

We retain server access logs for up to 90 days for security and debugging purposes. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months. LocalStorage data remains on your device until you delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Opt-out of advertising — opt out of personalised ads via Google Ads Settings.
  • Do Not Track — we honour Do Not Track browser signals where technically feasible.

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@webdesks.app.

9. Children's Privacy

WebDesks is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. International Users

WebDesks is operated from servers that may be located in the United States. If you are accessing WebDesks from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US where privacy laws may differ from your jurisdiction.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA): Our legal basis for processing the limited data we collect is our legitimate interest in operating and improving WebDesks, and compliance with advertising obligations. You may have additional rights under GDPR — contact us to exercise them.

11. Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect the WebDesks website. However, since your tool data is stored entirely in your browser's localStorage and never transmitted to us, the primary security responsibility for that data rests with your device and browser.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the date of the most recent revision at the top of this page. Continued use of WebDesks after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@webdesks.app
  • Contact form: webdesks.app/contact
