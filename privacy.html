Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how WebDesks ("we," "us," or "our"), operated at webdesks.app, collects, uses, and shares information when you visit or use our website. By using WebDesks, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at contact@thegreenborder.com.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

WebDesks is a browser-based productivity suite designed with privacy as a core principle. The following data is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Files you upload or process using the PDF Merger tool — all processing occurs locally in your browser using the pdf-lib JavaScript library. Your files never leave your device.
  • Recordings made using the Screen Recorder — video is captured and saved directly to your device via the browser's MediaRecorder API. Nothing is uploaded.
  • Tasks, notes, whiteboard drawings, timer settings, or any other content you create within the app — all data is stored in your browser's localStorage only.
  • Account credentials — WebDesks requires no account creation, login, or registration of any kind.

2. Information Automatically Collected

Like most websites, our hosting infrastructure may automatically log standard server access data including:

  • Your IP address (used for abuse prevention and server analytics)
  • Browser type and version
  • Referring page URL
  • Date and time of your visit
  • Pages visited and time spent

This data is retained in aggregate server logs for up to 90 days and is not linked to personally identifiable information. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise share this raw log data with third parties.

3. Cookies

WebDesks uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. Cookies are small text files stored in your browser by websites you visit. We use them in the following ways:

Functional / Essential Cookies

Some features of WebDesks may store small amounts of preference data in your browser's localStorage or session storage (such as your chosen color theme or timer settings). This is not technically a "cookie" but functions similarly — it persists data locally on your device for your convenience.

Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand aggregate usage patterns on WebDesks — how many visitors we receive, which tools are most popular, and how the site is navigated. Analytics cookies collect anonymized, aggregated information and do not identify you personally. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Third-Party Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense / DoubleClick)

WebDesks uses Google AdSense to display advertisements on this website. Google AdSense uses cookies — including the DoubleClick cookie — to serve ads that are relevant to your interests based on your browsing history across websites.

Specifically, Google may use the DoubleClick cookie to:

  • Deliver targeted advertisements based on your prior visits to WebDesks and other websites
  • Limit the number of times a specific advertisement is shown to you
  • Measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns
  • Prevent you from seeing the same ads repeatedly

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to WebDesks and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings or by visiting aboutads.info. For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site, please see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to WebDesks or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to WebDesks and/or other sites on the internet. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

4. Third-Party Services

WebDesks integrates with the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdSense — Advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — Typography (Inter, JetBrains Mono). Font files are served from Google's servers and Google may log font requests. Google Fonts Privacy FAQ
  • Tailwind CDN — Stylesheet delivery (cdn.tailwindcss.com)
  • Open-Meteo — Weather data for the Dashboard widget. Requests include your approximate geographic coordinates (if you grant location permission). Open-Meteo Terms
  • Quotable.io — Daily quotes displayed on the Dashboard. No personal data is sent in these requests.

5. Children's Privacy

WebDesks is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at contact@thegreenborder.com and we will take steps to delete such information.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access and deletion: Because WebDesks stores no personal data on our servers (your app data is in your browser's localStorage), you can delete all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser's site data for webdesks.app.
  • Cookie opt-out: You can disable cookies through your browser settings or use the opt-out links provided in Section 3 above.
  • GDPR (EU/EEA users): You have the right to object to the processing of your data for direct marketing purposes, including ad personalization. Use Google's Ads Settings to exercise this right for Google's advertising cookies.
  • CCPA (California users): California residents have the right to know about personal information collected, to request deletion, and to opt out of the "sale" of personal information. Because WebDesks does not sell personal information, the sale opt-out right is not applicable here. For questions, contact contact@thegreenborder.com.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect our website and services. Because WebDesks does not collect or store sensitive personal data on our servers, the risk of a data breach affecting your personal information is minimal. Your tool data (tasks, notes, files) resides exclusively in your browser and is never transmitted to us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of WebDesks after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
