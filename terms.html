🎥 Screen Recorder

Screen and audio recording uses your browser's built-in MediaRecorder API. Your recording is captured and stored temporarily in your browser's memory (RAM) and is never transmitted to any server.

Your responsibility: You are solely responsible for ensuring you have the legal right to record any screen content, including obtaining consent from others who may be visible or audible in your recordings. 🎬 Media Converter (WebM to MP4)

Video and image conversion is performed entirely in your browser using FFmpeg.wasm. Your media files are never uploaded to any server. Conversion happens locally on your device's CPU.

📝 Tasks, Notes & Other Tools

Your tasks, notes, scratchpad content, and preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device. It is not accessible to us and persists until you clear your browser's site data.
